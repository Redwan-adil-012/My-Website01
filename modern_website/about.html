Who I Am

My name is Radwan Ahmed Adil, and I come from Bangladesh. I am currently enrolled in the Web Design and Development course at Washington University of Science and Technology (WUST), which I am thoroughly enjoying.

One of the best things about my life is that I can gain power and understanding from times of reflection. Reading has been very important for me as a way to focus on myself, have fun, grow as a person, and change the way I see life.

I want to learn about both the technical and artistic sides of website design in this class so that I can make webpages that look good and work well. Over time, I want to get good enough at web development that I can lead creative online projects, work with driven teams, and follow instructions to provide useful digital solutions.

Looking for new ideas, being creative, and using new tools to improve my skills are some of my favorite things to do outside of school.

One interesting thing about me is that I can quickly adjust to new settings and find ideas in a lot of different places. I'm excited to start this journey and learn everything I need to know to shape my digital job path.

Thank you!
